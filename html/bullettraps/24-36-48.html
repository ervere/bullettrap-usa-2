Model BT 24, 36, 48

All features & benefits apply to the BT-36/50 BMG bullet trap.

Because of the thicker steel, this trap is exceptional for all firearms including a 50 BMG.

Features

Traps are made of long lasting, durable ballistic steel

Can endure handguns up to and including .44 Magnum, and rifles up to and including .223/5.56mm

Entire unit can be disassembled for install

Easily maintained

May Be Used For Many Prospects

  • Military or Police Personnel
  • Commercial or Residential
  • Gunsmith/Gun Shop Owners
  • Close Range Shooting for Tactical Training

Benefits

  • Provides for safe & clean target shooting indoors or outdoors
  • Environmentally Friendly - prevents contamination of soil from lead bullets
  • Trapped bullets are easily removed from collection pail - this is a clean way to recycle the spent bullets
  • No risk of ricochet or back splatter
  • Shoot more frequently
  • Affordable - low cost shooting solution
